# Length-Aware Rules

How the 7-part VSL structure compresses or expands depending on the target length. Read the section that matches your `video-meta.md` length.

## The Universal Rules

Regardless of length, these never change:
- The Hook is always present (3 seconds max)
- The CTA is always present (5-15 seconds)
- The Promise is always present (it's the reason to keep watching)
- The output language matches `video-meta.md`
- Spoken pace is ~150 words per minute

## 15-Second Format (Reels, TikTok, Shorts)

**Total budget**: ~37 words spoken

```
Part        Time     What's in / out
HOOK        3s       Required. Maximum intensity.
PROBLEM     -        CUT (no time)
PROMISE     5s       Required. One sentence only.
MECHANISM   -        CUT (or named in 2 words)
PROOF       2s       OPTIONAL: one number ("1,200 customers")
OFFER       -        CUT (link to landing page instead)
CTA         5s       Required. One verb, one destination.
```

**Strategy**: hook → promise → CTA. No proof, no mechanism, no offer details. The video is a teaser; the landing page does the rest.

**Example structure**:
> [HOOK 3s] "Stop chasing late invoices."
> [PROMISE 5s] "There's a way to lock client payments BEFORE you do the work."
> [PROOF 2s] "1,200+ businesses already do it."
> [CTA 5s] "Link below. 60 seconds to set up."

## 30-Second Format (Standard social ad)

**Total budget**: ~75 words spoken

```
Part        Time     What's in / out
HOOK        3s       Required.
PROBLEM     5s       OPTIONAL: one-sentence pain
PROMISE     5s       Required.
MECHANISM   5s       OPTIONAL: name only, no explanation
PROOF       3s       OPTIONAL: one number or one micro-testimonial
OFFER       5s       OPTIONAL: one bonus + guarantee mention
CTA         4s       Required.
```

**Strategy**: hook → quick pain → promise → name the mechanism → CTA. The optional beats fit if your hook+CTA are tight.

## 60-Second Format (Landing page hero, social ad)

**Total budget**: ~150 words spoken. **The sweet spot for landing pages.**

```
Part        Time     What's in / out
HOOK        3s       Required.
PROBLEM     10s      Required. Surface pain + one emotional layer.
PROMISE     10s      Required. Specific dream outcome.
MECHANISM   12s      Required. Name + one-sentence definition.
PROOF       10s      Required. One number + one micro-testimonial.
OFFER       10s      Required. Top bonus + guarantee.
CTA         5s       Required.
```

**Strategy**: full structure but every beat is tight. No room for second proof points or extended offer reveals. Choose the strongest of each.

## 90-Second Format (Explainer)

**Total budget**: ~225 words spoken.

```
Part        Time     What's in / out
HOOK        3s       Required.
PROBLEM     15s      Surface pain + Layer 3 drilldown.
PROMISE     12s      Outcome + identity shift.
MECHANISM   18s      Name + definition + one differentiator.
PROOF       18s      One number + one short named testimonial.
OFFER       15s      Top 2 bonuses + guarantee.
CTA         9s       Verb + destination + one urgency note.
```

**Strategy**: room to develop one beat slightly. Often the Mechanism gets the extra time because it's the differentiator that justifies the click.

## 3-5 Minute Format (Mid-form sales video)

**Total budget**: 450-750 words spoken.

```
Part        Time     What's in / out
HOOK        3s       Required.
PROBLEM     30-60s   Surface pain + drilldown to Layer 5 + failed attempts.
PROMISE     20-40s   Outcome + identity + status shift.
MECHANISM   60-90s   Name + definition + how it works + contrast with competitors.
PROOF       45-90s   2-3 named testimonials with specific results, plus one number/data point.
OFFER       30-60s   Full stack: 3-5 bonuses, guarantee, urgency.
CTA         15s      Verb + destination + repeat (twice).
```

**Strategy**: full development of every beat. Room for one objection-handling moment in the offer beat ("I know you're thinking X. Here's why that's not the case.")

## 10+ Minute Format (Long-form VSL)

**Total budget**: 1500+ words spoken.

```
Part        Time     What's in / out
HOOK        3s       Required.
PROBLEM     2-3 min  Full pain story with avatar identification, drilldown, failed attempts, and one emotional crescendo.
PROMISE     1-2 min  Outcome + identity + status + a specific "imagine" scene.
MECHANISM   3-4 min  Full reveal: name, definition, how, why, why-not-others, the principle behind it.
PROOF       3-5 min  3-5 named testimonials, multiple data points, possibly a live demo.
OFFER       2-4 min  Full stack with bonus reveals one at a time, guarantee with framing, urgency with reason, price reveal at the end.
CTA         30-60s   Verb + destination + repeat 3 times + final urgency note.
```

**Strategy**: this is a sales letter on video. Pacing is slower, breathing room is intentional, the audience has self-selected to watch a long video.

## How To Pick the Length

| If your channel is... | Use this length |
|---|---|
| Reels / TikTok / Shorts | 15-30s |
| Instagram feed video | 30-60s |
| Landing page hero | 60-90s |
| YouTube explainer | 90s-3 min |
| Webinar / sales page | 3-5 min |
| Cold-traffic VSL | 10-30 min |

## The Compression Rule

When in doubt, go shorter. A tight 60s video outperforms a flabby 90s video almost every time. The Hook and CTA always have to be there; everything else fights for the remaining space.
